Before you scout

Rigging a highline is dangerous and can be fatal.

Finding and assessing a spot is the most dangerous part of highlining: this tool only points to terrain that might hold a line — it never confirms solid anchors, safe access, rock quality, or that a line can be rigged.

You alone are responsible for assessing any spot and for your own safety. RemoteScout accepts no liability for any injury, death, or damage arising from its use.
